About Sr. Paul McCartney


Paul McCartney is an English singer, songwriter, musician, and record and film producer.
McCartney gained his worldwide fame as co-lead vocalist and bassist for the Beatles band.
After the group disbanded in 1970, he pursued a solo career and formed the band Wings with his first wife, Linda, and Denny Laine.
A self-taught musician, McCartney is proficient on bass, guitar, keyboards, and drums.
McCartney began his career as a member of the Quarrymen in 1957, which evolved into the Beatles in 1960.
His Beatles songs "And I Love Her" (1964), "Yesterday" (1965), "Eleanor Rigby" (1966) and "Blackbird" (1968) rank among the most covered songs in history.

Q&A


Q1: Main diffrences between web1.0, web2.0 and web3.0:


1. Compares to web 1 and web 3, which uses web crawlers- In web1 you dont have crawlers that search for web pages.
2. Web1 pages are static, compared to web2 and web 3, which their pages are dynamic for changes.
3. In web1 we can search online only with the specific URL we are looking for.
In web2 we can serach online using a search engine (with tags).
In web3 we can search online using a search engine (that uses a semantic search).



Q2: The query "pills side effects":


In web1: we will have to use the specific URL of a site that talks about "pills side effects".
In web2: we can search the query. We will get sites that contains the tags "pills", "side", "effects" (not necessary together).
In web3: we can search the query. We will get sites that connect to the topic of the query (and not necessary contains the specific words)
